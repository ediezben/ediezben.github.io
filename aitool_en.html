Artificial Intelligence solutions and tools have an internal impact on the results of company processes.

The processes are sequenced sets of workflows, made up of simpler activities, which pursue a specific objective, which in turn will contribute to the attainment of the specific objectives of the business, with the ultimate goal of attaining the general objectives of the company.

There are some processes that are present in practically all companies, regardless of their sector. We call these processes in our application general or common business processes, amongst others they include processes of general management, marketing and human resources.

Unlike them, there are other processes that only appear in certain sectors, examples of which would be soil control in agriculture, livestock health management in livestock business, virtual sales assistance in retail and others.

Now, in the AI ​​impact assessment project you are carrying out, what process do you want to target? Choose a process to descend into your workflows and the specific activities that will be affected by the AI ​​solution. Choose between the options of the drop-down menus that we present to you.
